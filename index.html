What are the rules?

1. To be eligible for the competition, you need to be a Boosteroid active subscriber.

2. Record your gameplay on Boosteroid desktop apps using the built-in Clip Recording feature.

3. Publish your videos on YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook or Twitter/X open to the public with the hashtag #boosteroid_spotlight.

4. You can publish as many spotlights as you want, each of your contributions will be counted during the final draw. More videos - more chances of winning.

5. Join our Discord to watch the live giveaway event and see who the lucky winners are.

6. If you win, expect a DM from our team on the social media platform where you posted the videos.

7. In case the winner of the GAMESCOM trip cannot use the prize, we will offer to swap prizes with the other winners. If they do not agree, we will redraw the prize on a new live stream.

8. You cannot get a money equivalent to the prize you have won.

9. The deadline to publish videos to take part in the giveaway is Tuesday, July 30, 23:59 UTC.

10. The winners will be announced during a live draw on Wednesday, July 31, 12:00 UTC (all details can be found on Discord).
